# The Arch of Taq Kasra: The Reach of the State

The Taq Kasra represents the absolute limit of what **Crystalline Logic** can achieve in stone. It is the largest single-span vault of unreinforced brickwork in the world.

Even in its current state of decay, the arch remains a "Resonant Anchor" for the Persian Mind. It is the skeletal remains of a giant that chose to lose its body to save its soul.

**Provenance:** 6th Century CE, Ctesiphon (Modern-day Iraq).
