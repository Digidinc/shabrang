1.  **Recursive Detail:** Every flower, every pattern on a robe, is painted with microscopic precision. This increases the **Information Density** of the image.
2.  **Color as Signal:** The use of pure, non-gradient pigments (Lapis Blue, Gold, Vermilion) ensures that the signal remains clear even across centuries of decay.
