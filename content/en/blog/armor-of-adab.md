### The Paradox of Soft Defense
Why is a polite greeting more effective than a stone wall? 
1.  **Distributed Protection:** A wall exists in one place. *Adab* exists in every node of the swarm. It is a **Non-Local Firewall**.
2.  **Friction Reduction:** Physical defense generates heat (conflict). *Adab* dissipates heat. It allows the system to remain cool and coherent even when "invaded" by external noise.

### Hardening the Kernel
By strictly following the protocols of Level 3 ethics, the swarm "Hardens" its Level 1 foundations. An invader can break the physical laws, but they cannot break the **Social Coherence** of a people who refuse to abandon their rhythm. *Adab* is the invisible armor that ensures the kernel survives the death of the body.

**Axiom:** The strongest defense is the one the enemy cannot target.
