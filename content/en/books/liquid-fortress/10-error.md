## Cultural Anchors & Verses

### The Unity of Being: Avicenna (Ibn Sina)

Against the Manichaean error of splitting the world into two substances, Avicenna proposes a single, unified chain of existence.

> "The soul and the body are not two enemies locked in a cage; they are two aspects of a single reality. The soul is the perfection of the body, and the body is the instrument of the soul's awakening."  
> — **Avicenna**, *The Book of Healing (Al-Shifa)*

### The Painter's Lie: Classical Folklore

Mani was celebrated as a master painter, but his art was seen as a "Deception" that drew the eye away from the goodness of creation.

> "Mani drew a heaven that was not of this earth,  
> And a hell that made the soil feel like a prison.  
> He painted the light as a captive of the dark,  
> Forgetting that the shadows are but the play of the sun."  
> — *Medieval Persian Commentary*
