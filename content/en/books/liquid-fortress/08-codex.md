## Global Resonance & Zeitgeist

The mainstream historical zeitgeist admires the **Sasanian Empire** for its administrative genius, which later served as the blueprint for the Abbasid Caliphate. Scholars like **Max Weber** would see in the Sasanian state an early "Rational-Legal" bureaucracy.

The Sovereign perspective warns that this was a case of **Over-Coherence**. When a system becomes a "Crystalline Lattice," it loses its ability to flow. The Sasanian *Codex* was so perfect that it became brittle. This aligns with modern **Complexity Theory**, which states that "Antifragility" (the ability to gain from disorder) requires a degree of internal "messiness" that the Sasanians had purged.
