## Global Resonance & Zeitgeist

The contemporary academic world, influenced by the **Sapir-Whorf Hypothesis** (Linguistic Relativity), recognizes that language is the primary architect of a culture's worldview. The zeitgeist views language preservation as a form of "cultural resistance."

The Sovereign perspective identifies the Persian mother tongue as a **Linguistic Ark**. While the public square (*Biruni*) was overwritten by Arabic, the *Andaruni* remained a "clean room" where the original operating system was kept in incubation. This aligns with **Evolutionary Biology**, where a species' survival often depends on "Refugia"—small, isolated areas where life persists while the surrounding environment is hostile.
