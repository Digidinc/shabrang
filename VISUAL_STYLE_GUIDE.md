# Shabrang Visual Style Guide

**Source:** NotebookLM prompts used for slide generation
**Style:** Persian miniature aesthetic (flat, no 3D)
**Purpose:** All visual content (slides, social graphics, book illustrations)

## Color Palette (Strict)

### Background Colors
- **Warm cream/aged parchment:** `#F5E6C8`
- **Burnished gold:** `#C9A84C`

### Thematic Colors

**Coal black** (Primary)
- Hex: `#1A1A18`
- Use: Outlines, figures, text
- Symbolism: Structure, definition, grounding

**Deep teal** (Coherence/Water/Flow)
- Hex: `#1A4A4A` to `#2D5A6B`
- Symbolism: The qanat, hidden streams, coherence fields
- FRC connection: Probability density manifolds, flow states

**Persian crimson** (Entropy/Fire/Danger)
- Hex: `#8B3535` to `#8B3A3A`
- Symbolism: Chaos, transformation, boundaries
- FRC connection: Entropy gradients, resonance disruption

**Antique gold** (Sacred/Light/Value)
- Hex: `#C9A227` to `#D4A84B`
- Symbolism: Wisdom, value, illumination
- FRC connection: Information density, coherence peaks

**Garden green** (Nature/Growth)
- Hex: `#3D5C3D`
- Use: Sparingly (accent only)
- Symbolism: Life, emergence, organic systems

## Composition Rules

### Persian Miniature Aesthetic
1. **Flat perspective** - NO 3D rendering, NO depth simulation
2. **Clean black ink outlines** on all figures
3. **Solid color fills** - NO gradients, NO glow effects, NO neon
4. **High contrast** - designed for print readability
5. **Symmetrical or balanced layouts** preferred

### Framing
- **Border:** Simple geometric Persian border frame
- **Border colors:** Teal + gold combination
- **Border style:** Clean lines, geometric patterns

### Typography (Implied)
- High contrast black text on cream/gold backgrounds
- Persian-inspired calligraphic elements when appropriate
- Readable at distance (presentation-ready)

## Design Rationale

### Why Persian Miniature Style?

1. **Cultural Authenticity** - Aligns with Persian identity themes in The Liquid Fortress
2. **Timelessness** - Flat, iconic style ages well (vs. trendy 3D renders)
3. **Clarity** - High contrast, simple forms communicate complex ideas clearly
4. **Print-Ready** - Works in black/white photocopies if needed
5. **Distinctive** - Stands out from generic AI-generated imagery

### Why These Colors?

**Teal (Coherence/Water):**
- Persian architecture (tiles, mosaics)
- Water as metaphor for hidden infrastructure (qanat)
- FRC coherence fields, probability flows

**Crimson (Entropy/Fire):**
- Persian carpets, textiles
- Fire as transformation, danger, boundaries
- FRC entropy gradients, disruption

**Gold (Sacred/Value):**
- Persian illuminated manuscripts
- Light as wisdom, value, meaning
- FRC information density, resonance peaks

**Cream/Parchment (Background):**
- Aged manuscripts, timeless knowledge
- Warm, inviting, readable
- Print-friendly

**Black (Structure):**
- Calligraphy, definition, grounding
- FRC solid level foundation
- Architectural clarity

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
✅ Use exact hex codes from palette
✅ Maintain high contrast (black on cream/gold)
✅ Use teal for coherence/water concepts
✅ Use crimson for entropy/fire concepts
✅ Use gold for sacred/value concepts
✅ Frame all graphics with teal + gold border
✅ Keep perspective flat (Persian miniature)
✅ Use solid color fills only
✅ Make text readable at distance

### Don'ts
❌ Mix in colors outside the palette
❌ Use gradients or glow effects
❌ Add 3D perspective or depth
❌ Use photorealistic textures
❌ Overuse garden green (accent only)
❌ Create busy, cluttered compositions
❌ Use low-contrast color combinations
❌ Add neon or fluorescent effects

## Content Type → Color Emphasis

### Blog Posts
- **Header graphic:** Cream background, gold frame, black text
- **Featured image:** Teal or crimson dominant (based on theme)
- **Diagrams:** Flat, geometric, 2-3 colors max

### Social Media
- **Instagram:** Square 1080x1080, gold border, large text
- **Twitter:** Landscape 1200x675, simplified composition
- **Facebook:** Flexible, but maintain palette
- **Telegram:** Vertical 800x1200, readable on mobile

### Presentations
- **Title slides:** Burnished gold background `#C9A84C`, black text
- **Content slides:** Cream background `#F5E6C8`, black text
- **Diagrams:** Teal + crimson + gold, black outlines
- **Quotes:** Gold background, black text, teal border

### Book Interior
- **Chapter openers:** Full-page illustration, Persian miniature style
- **Section breaks:** Geometric border patterns, gold + teal
- **Diagrams:** Flat, iconic, FRC concepts simplified
- **Quotes:** Gold background boxes, black text

## Multilingual Considerations

### Persian Text (Farsi)
- Use high-quality Persian calligraphic fonts
- Maintain right-to-left text flow
- Ensure text is readable at small sizes
- Gold + black combination for elegance

### English Text
- Use serif fonts for book content
- Sans-serif for diagrams and social media
- Maintain same color palette
- High contrast for readability

### Arabic Text
- Similar to Persian (right-to-left)
- Calligraphic style for headers
- Clean, readable body text
